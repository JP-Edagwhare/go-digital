SMEDAN stands for Small and Medium-scale Enterprise Development Agency of Nigeria. SMEDAN was established to promote the development of the MSME sector of the Nigeria Economy. Also, to establish a structured and efficient micro, small and medium enterprises sector that will enhance sustainable economic development of Nigeria.

There are many benefits of registering with SMEDAN including receiving a SMEDAN certificate with embedded SMEDAN Unique Identification Number (SUIN) confirming their business status; access to grants, loans and other financial services through SMEDAN or in partnership with other development agencies and access to capacity building, training and other learning opportunities for the business owners and employees.

CAC is Corporate Affairs Commission established to administer the Act, including the regulation and supervision of the formation, incorporation, management and winding up of companies. While TIN is Taxpayer Identification Number is an identification number used by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) in the administration of tax laws. It is issued either by the Social Security Administration (SSA) or by the IRS.
